Heredity

This project explores using Bayesian networks to model genetic inheritance. We focus on a hearing impairment gene (GJB2) with mutations causing deafness. The model considers a family: parents with Gene and Trait variables, and a child inheriting genes with a chance of mutation. The goal is to use this model to infer gene probabilities and predict hearing impairment likelihood in a population, given information about individuals and their families.
